B-Tech

B. Tech is an undergraduate level programme that is pursued by science stream students after the completion of Class 12 Science. The four year programme is offered by CV RAMAN GLOBAL UNIVERSITY.

M-Tech

MTech is a professional postgraduate engineering master degree programme awarded to candidates after completion of two years of study in the discipline of engineering. This degree is predicated in a specific branch of engineering.

Diploma

CV Raman Global University provide Diploma Course that testifies that the recipient has successfully completed a particular course of study.

MBA

A master of business administration (MBA) is a graduate degree that provides theoretical and practical training for business or investment management.


Polytechnic

Polytechnics offers diploma in engineering to students who have completed their 10th qualifying examination. The duration of the course is 3 years and the students will be given technical education in CV Raman Global University.

M.Sc

The Master of Science (M.Sc.) is an academic degree for a post-graduate candidates or researchers, it usually takes from 4 to 7 years after passing the Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) degree. That is Offered By CV Raman Global University.
